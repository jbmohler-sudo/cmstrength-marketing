About Center Mass Strength

Center Mass Strength is built on a simple philosophy: serious strength athletes deserve real programming, not recycled templates. Our adaptive algorithms respond to your readiness, fatigue levels, weak points, and competition dates.

The Center Mass Method

Most strength apps are generic templates dressed up in new interfaces. They don't think. They don't adapt. Center Mass Strength is different. The Center Mass Method uses real algorithmic programming, RPE-based autoregulation, and competition-date-aware periodization to build programs that improve your lifts week by week.

Real Algorithms

No cookie-cutter templates. Our engine calculates your readiness, adjusts intensity, and prescribes weights based on your actual performance data—not generic percentages.

Block Periodization

Rotate through focused training blocks—strength, hypertrophy, dynamic effort—to develop multiple qualities simultaneously while preventing plateaus and managing fatigue.

Autoregulation

Your recovery fluctuates day to day. Our readiness engine assesses how you're feeling—fatigue, sleep, stress—and adjusts your session intensity in real time.

Competition-Date Aware

If you have a meet date, our macrocycle engine builds backward from that date. Every week has purpose. Every block has a role.

Weak Point Specialization

Identify what's holding you back—off-the-floor, lockout, stalling mid-bench—and our engine prioritizes the variations and accessories that will fix it.

For Serious Lifters

This app is built for people who care about their numbers. If you're training for strength, this is your operating system.

Our Programs

Center Mass Strength offers three tracks, each with its own engine behavior and training philosophy:

Bench Specialization

For lifters focused on pressing strength. We prioritize bench press development, manage weak points (lockout, bottom-of-press, shoulder stability), and coordinate secondary movements that support bench gains. Perfect for meets where bench is your strength, or for athletes who want to build a competition-quality press.

Jacked & Strong

The sweet spot between muscle and strength. Alternates 5-week hypertrophy blocks with 4-week strength blocks. You get the arm and back development of bodybuilding-adjacent training, but the strength gains and competitive spirit of powerlifting.

SBD (Full Powerlifting)

Competition-focused periodization for the full squat-bench-deadlift total. Works backward from your meet date, building a macrocycle that peaks all three lifts for maximum total at competition. Includes weak point identification and lift-specific variation strategies.
